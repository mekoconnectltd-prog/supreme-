Configured Processing Flow

Customer opens GitHub Pages app
        ↓
Clicks "Buy now – Secure checkout"
        ↓
Frontend sends POST to Vercel /api/create-checkout
        ↓
Stripe Checkout session is created
        ↓
Customer completes payment on Stripe
        ↓
Stripe sends checkout.session.completed to /api/webhook
        ↓
Backend verifies webhook and creates transfers
        ↓
Customer returns to ?payment=success or ?payment=cancel
